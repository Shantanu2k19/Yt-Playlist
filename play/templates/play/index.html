Dont Know The Video YouTube Deleted?

YouTube delete videos without telling the users who have saved those videos in their playlists. You can't even know the title of video that was deleted and hence can't find a replacement.
MeTube helps you to track the title of deleted videos.

Watch Video

Track

WeTube uses YouTube's API to know about the playlists of the user. It saves the data and can only be accessed by the user itself. Once the video is deleted, user can log into their account to find the Title of the video and can save a replacement in their plylist.

WebApp

User can Log into WeTube web application and find the video title.

Chrome Extension

A chrome extension to notify and easy access will be provided soon to the users.

Privacy

The data is not shared with any 3rd party application and WeTube being open source, the trust is maintained by the open source community.

Security

Using YouTube's API, the login functionality is handeled by youtube itself with highest priority to security.

Our Right

"User have the right to know atleast the title of video that got deleted"
